66App User Manual

Your guide to fashion, shopping, and community on 66

Welcome

Welcome to 66App — a fashion-focused social platform where you can discover outfit inspiration, follow your favourite creators, and shop the products they recommend, all in one place.

Whether you're here to browse the latest trends, share your own style, or earn rewards through affiliate links, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

Sign Up & Sign In

When you first open 66App you'll see the login screen. You can switch between Sign In (returning users) and Sign Up (new users) using the toggle at the top.

Creating an Account

  1. Open the app and tap the Sign Up tab.
  2. Choose a sign-in method:
    • Continue with Apple
    • Continue with Google
    • Continue with LINE
  3. Follow the prompts from your chosen provider to authorise 66App.
  4. Once complete, you'll be taken straight to your Home Feed.

Signing Back In

  1. Open the app and tap the Sign In tab.
  2. Tap the same provider you used when you signed up.
  3. You'll be signed in automatically and returned to your Home Feed.
Tip
You can switch the app language between English and Thai right from the login screen using the toggle in the top-right corner.

Navigating the App

66App uses a bottom navigation bar with four main tabs:

TabWhat It Does
HomeYour personal feed of posts from people you follow
CreateShare a new outfit post (opens the post creator)
ExploreBrowse trending posts from the entire community
ProfileView your profile, stats, and achievements

Tap any tab to jump to that section. You can always return to the Home Feed by tapping Home.

Home Feed

The Home Feed is the first screen you see after signing in. It shows posts from people you follow, with the most recent posts at the top.

Browsing Posts

  • Scroll down to see more posts — new posts load automatically as you reach the bottom.
  • Pull down from the top of the screen to refresh your feed.
  • Tap any post image to open the full post detail.

Post Card

Each post in your feed shows:

  • The creator's profile picture, display name, and handle
  • The post image
  • Like, comment, and save buttons
  • The number of likes
  • A preview of the caption
  • A link to view comments

Explore & Discover

The Explore tab shows trending posts from the entire 66App community in a two-column photo grid. It's the best way to discover new creators and outfit ideas.

  • Scroll through the grid to browse content.
  • Tap any image to open the full post.
  • Each tile shows the creator's handle and a short caption.
  • New posts load automatically as you scroll.

Creating a Post

Share your outfits and style with the community by creating a post.

  1. Tap the Create button in the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Choose your image source:
    • Photo Library — pick an image from your device
    • Take Photo — snap a new photo with your camera
  3. Review your image preview. Tap it to choose a different image.
  4. Write a caption (up to 2,200 characters). A character counter helps you stay within the limit.
  5. Tap Share in the top-right corner to publish your post.
Tip
For the best quality, use images that are at least 1080px wide. The app supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats.

Editing & Deleting Posts

Editing a Caption

  1. Open one of your own posts.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Select Edit caption.
  4. Update your caption text and tap Save.

Deleting a Post

  1. Open one of your own posts.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Select Delete post.
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
Warning
Deleting a post is permanent and cannot be undone. All likes, comments, and affiliate links associated with the post will also be removed.

Liking Posts

Show appreciation for posts you enjoy by tapping the heart icon on any post card or on the post detail screen. Tap it again to unlike.

The total number of likes is displayed next to the heart icon.

Commenting

Join the conversation by leaving comments on posts.

  1. Open a post and tap the comment icon, or tap "View all comments".
  2. The comments panel slides up from the bottom of the screen.
  3. Type your comment in the text field at the bottom.
  4. Tap the send button to post your comment.

You can tap on any commenter's name or avatar to visit their profile.

Tip
Some comments may be pinned by the post creator — these appear with a "Pinned" badge and are shown prominently.

Saving Posts

Save posts you want to revisit later by tapping the bookmark icon on any post card or post detail screen. Tap it again to unsave.

You can find all your saved posts from your profile. See Saved Posts for more details.

Shopping from Posts

Many posts on 66App include product links that let you shop the exact items shown in an outfit.

  1. Open a post that has product links — they appear below the caption in the "Affiliate links" section.
  2. Each link shows the product name and price (if available).
  3. Tap a link to open the product page in your browser.
Tip
Shopping through product links on 66App supports the creators who styled the outfit. It's a great way to show your appreciation!

For Creators: Adding Product Links

If you have a creator account, you can add affiliate product links to your posts so your followers can shop the items you feature.

  1. Open one of your own posts.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu and select Add affiliate link.
  3. Fill in the link details:
    • Destination URL (required) — the product page link
    • Product title — a name for the product
    • Product image URL — a thumbnail of the product
    • Price and Currency — e.g., 990 THB
  4. Tap Add link to save. The link will appear on your post immediately.

You can add multiple product links to a single post.

Creator Analytics Dashboard

As a creator, you have access to an analytics dashboard that shows how your product links are performing.

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon on your profile).
  2. Tap Creator Analytics.

The dashboard shows you how many times your affiliate links have been clicked, helping you understand which products resonate with your audience.

Tip
The Creator Analytics option only appears if you have a creator account. If you don't see it, contact support to upgrade your account.

Viewing Your Profile

Tap the Profile tab to see your own profile. Your profile page includes:

  • Your avatar, display name, and bio
  • Your stats: number of posts, followers, and people you follow
  • Quick-access buttons: Edit Profile and Saved
  • Your niche tags (if you've set any)
  • Your level, XP progress, and streak information
  • A grid of all your posts

You can also view other people's profiles by tapping their name or avatar anywhere in the app.

Editing Your Profile

Personalise your profile to reflect your style.

  1. Go to your Profile tab and tap Edit Profile, or go to Settings and tap Edit Profile.
  2. Update any of the following fields:
FieldDetails
Display NameYour public name (required, up to 100 characters)
HandleYour unique @username (required, 3–30 characters, letters/numbers/underscores only)
PronounsChoose from he/him, she/her, they/them, or enter custom pronouns
BioTell people about yourself (up to 500 characters)
GenderMale, Female, or Other
LinksAdd up to 5 website URLs to your profile
Profile SongLink a Spotify or Apple Music track to your profile

Tap Save when you're done. Your changes will be visible to everyone immediately.

Followers & Following

From your profile (or anyone else's), tap the Followers or Following count to see the full list.

Each entry shows the person's avatar, display name, and handle. Tap any entry to visit their profile.

Saved Posts

Access your bookmarked posts at any time:

  1. Go to your Profile tab.
  2. Tap the Saved button.

You'll see a grid of all the posts you've saved. Tap any post to open it.

Levels & XP

66App rewards you with experience points (XP) as you use the app. You earn XP by creating posts, engaging with content, and being active on the platform.

  • Your current level and XP progress bar are displayed on your profile.
  • The progress bar shows how much XP you need to reach the next level.
  • Levelling up unlocks new achievements and recognition within the community.

Streaks

Keep your streak alive by opening 66App every day! Your streak counter is displayed on your profile with a flame icon.

  • Each consecutive day you use the app adds one to your streak.
  • If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero.
Tip
Building a long streak can help you earn special achievements. Try to check in daily!

Badges & Achievements

Earn badges and achievements as you reach milestones on 66App.

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon on your profile).
  2. Tap Achievements.

The achievements screen lists all available badges and shows which ones you've unlocked. Keep creating, engaging, and exploring to collect them all!

Language & Appearance

Language

66App supports English and Thai. To change your language:

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon on your profile).
  2. In the Language section, tap your preferred language.

Appearance

Choose how the app looks:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. In the Appearance section, choose:
    • Light — always light mode
    • Dark — always dark mode
    • System — follows your device setting

Your choice is applied immediately.

Account Information

View your account details in Settings:

  • Email — the email address from your sign-in provider (read-only)
  • Account status — shows whether your account is active
Tip
Your email is managed by the sign-in provider you used (Apple, Google, or LINE). To change it, update it with your provider directly.

Deleting Your Account

If you'd like to delete your 66App account:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down to the Danger Zone and tap Delete Account.
  3. Read the warning message carefully.
  4. Type DELETE (all uppercase, exactly as shown) in the confirmation field.
  5. Tap the Delete Account button to confirm.
Important
After you confirm, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion. You have a 30-day grace period during which you can contact support to restore your account. After 30 days, all your data — posts, comments, likes, and profile information — will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Signing Out

To sign out of 66App:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap the red Sign Out button.

You'll be returned to the login screen. You can sign back in at any time using the same provider.

Reporting Content

If you see a post that violates community guidelines, you can report it:

  1. Open the post and tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  2. Select Report.
  3. Choose a reason:
    • Spam
    • Inappropriate content
    • Harassment
    • Other
  4. Tap Submit.

Our team will review the report and take appropriate action. You'll see a confirmation message once your report has been submitted.

Blocking Users

You can also report users directly from their profile page using the same reporting flow. If someone is bothering you, reporting them helps our moderation team keep the community safe.

Tip
All reports are confidential. The reported user will not know who submitted the report.
